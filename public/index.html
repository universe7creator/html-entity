Frequently Asked Questions

What are HTML entities?

HTML entities are special codes used to represent characters that have special meaning in HTML (like <, >, and &) or characters that aren't available on standard keyboards (like ©, ®, and €).

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All encoding and decoding happens in your browser. Your text is never sent to our servers or stored anywhere.

What entity formats are supported?

We support all standard HTML entity formats: named entities (&amp;), decimal entities (&#38;), and hexadecimal entities (&#x26;).
